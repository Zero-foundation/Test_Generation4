Fibonacci.

Ingredients.
0 g fiba
1 g fibb
16 g iterator
16 g second iterator

Method.
Chop the iterator.
Put fibb into 1st mixing bowl.
Put fibb into 1st mixing bowl.
Add fiba into 1st mixing bowl.
Fold fibb into 1st mixing bowl.
Fold fiba into 1st mixing bowl.
Put fiba into 1st mixing bowl.
Chop the iterator until choped.
Mash the second iterator.
Fold fiba into 1st mixing bowl.
Put fiba into 2nd mixing bowl.
Mash the second iterator until mashed.
Pour contents of the 2nd mixing bowl into the 1st baking dish.

Serves 1.
